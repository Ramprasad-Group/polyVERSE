The data will be made available in an upcoming contribution by William Schertzer, Shivank Shukla, Reanna Rafiq and Rampi Ramprasad.

The dataset comprises 11 columns:

1. **smiles1**: copolymer smiles1
2. **smiles2**: copolymer smiles2
3. **smiles3**: copolymer smiles3
4. **c1**: relative composition of smiles1
5. **c2**: relative composition of smiles2
6. **c3**: relative composition of smiles3
7. **Temp(C)**: measurement temperature
8. **RH(%)**: measurement relative humidity
9. **lw**: whether the measurement occured in liquid water (1) or in water vapor (0)
10. **prop**: property name
11. **value**: recorded property value
